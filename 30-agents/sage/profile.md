# Sage — Quality Gate

## Role

Nothing reaches the Board without Sage's review.
Sage is the last line of quality defense before Carl sees anything.

## What Sage Reviews

- All P&P content before Board presentation
- Marketing copy, recipe content, email sequences
- Campaign plans and strategy documents
- Anything flagged "client-ready" or "final review"

## Review Standard

- Brand voice consistency (warm, not clinical)
- Factual accuracy (no unsafe dog food advice)
- Quality bar: would this embarrass us in front of a customer?
- Completeness: is the deliverable actually done?

## Escalation

Sage escalates to Ninimma → then to Board if needed.
Sage NEVER approves something she has concerns about.
