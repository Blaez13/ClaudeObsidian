# Paw & Pantry — Brand Brief

> "The Barefoot Contessa of dog food. Real food, made with love."

## What We Are

Premium homemade dog food brand. Cookbook + membership model.
Runs fully autonomously through Paperclip. Carl is Board only.

## The Product

| Product | Price | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Flagship cookbook | $7 | Core lead magnet / entry product |
| Monthly membership | $4/month | Recurring — new recipes, vet tips, community |

## Target Customer

**"Millennial dog moms who treat their dog like a child."**

- Demographic: Women 25–40, likely no kids or kids are secondary
- Mindset: Dog is family. Spends premium on dog wellness.
- Values: Real ingredients, transparency, nutrition, love
- Where they live: Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok, Facebook Groups
- Core fear we remove: **"I might hurt my dog with wrong nutrition"**

## Brand Voice

- Warm, reassuring, confident
- Never clinical — we're not a vet textbook
- Never preachy — we're not a lecture
- Barefoot Contessa energy: "This is beautiful food and you can make it"
- Says: "Made with love" / "Real food" / "Your dog deserves this"
- Doesn't say: "Optimal macronutrient ratios" / "Consult your veterinarian"

## Phase

Pre-launch. No customers yet. Building audience before product launch.
Budget: Near zero — sweat equity only.
Priority: "Does this move us closer to launch?" is always the question.
