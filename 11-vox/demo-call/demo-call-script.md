## What Makes a Great Take

DO:
- Sound natural, slightly uncertain (real customers sound this way)
- Let pauses happen — do not rush to fill them
- React authentically to what Annie-Beth says ("Oh, okay" / "Uh-huh")
- Let the booking confirmation land before you respond
- Ask the "are you AI?" question naturally — this is the money moment

DON'T:
- Sound like you are reading a script
- Talk too fast
- Interrupt Annie-Beth mid-sentence
- Use a fake name/number that might confuse GHL

## Ideal Call Length
60–90 seconds is the sweet spot. Long enough to show the full flow.
Short enough to use as a social clip without editing.

If the call runs 90+ seconds because of natural conversation, that is fine.
We can trim for clips. We cannot fix rushed or robotic audio.
